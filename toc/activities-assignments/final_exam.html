Final Examination

University of Waterloo Senate-approved academic regulations related to assignments, tests, and final exams can be found in the current Undergraduate Studies Academic Calendar. 

Description

The Final Examination will consist of multiple choice questions. Before the end of the semester, I will review both the format of the exam and the material for which you are responsible. A short practice exam with example questions will be released three weeks prior to the exam.

Final Examination Arrangements and Schedule

If your final exam will be held in-person, you will need to confirm or make arrangements to write it. Depending on your location and circumstances, your exam may be scheduled on a Waterloo campus, at an external exam centre, or with a proctor.

You can find the information about your exam arrangements (including dates, exam centre locations, how to find a proctor and submit proctor information, etc.) in the Write Exams section on the Centre for Extended Learning website. Please do this as soon as possible.
