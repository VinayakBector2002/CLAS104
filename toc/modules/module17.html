Module 17

Nostoi  and the Wonders of Odysseus’ Journey

Objectives:

  • Examine the ancient myth of Odysseus’ fantastical journey home to Ithaca from Troy and ancient narratives of return.

In this module, we will examine the myth of Odysseus' return home from Troy to his wife Penelope. This voyage, which took ten years, contains many fantastical elements that are very close to folktale myths we will examine later in the course. When Odysseus returns home, he must regain his rightful place as the ruler of his homeland Ithaca. We will also in this module consider more generally the class of 'return' myths, known in Greek as Nostoi.
