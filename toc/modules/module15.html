Module 15

Mycenaean Saga

Objectives:

  • Explore the sagas in mythology that focus on the city of Mycenae and relate to the Trojan war.

The Mycenaean saga is linked to the important Bronze Age city Mycenae and focuses on family of Atreus. In its later generations, this family is integrally connected to the story of the Trojan War which we will look at next week. The king Agamemnon, for example, who leads the Greek at Troy, is a king of Mycenae.
