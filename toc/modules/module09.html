Module 09

Hermes

Objectives:

  • Explore the nature and function of the god Hermes/Mercury and central myths involving him.

We are going to look in detail at several myths that surround the god Hermes, including the story of his birth and adolescence told in the long Homeric Hymn devoted to him.
