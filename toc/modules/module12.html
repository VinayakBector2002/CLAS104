Module 12

Orpheus

Objectives:

  • Explore the myth of the poet-singer Orpheus and his tragic love affair.

In the second half of the course, we will begin by looking at the Greek conceptions of death and the underworld in mythology. As a step in that direction, we are going to look at a human character who makes a very famous descent into the underworld and has a special relationship with the divine. The motif of descent into the underworld is a popular one in Classical mythology; not only goddesses such as Persephone, but heroes and other semi-divine characters descend into the underworld for various purposes.

Essential Element: Full
