Module 01

What is Mythology?

Objectives:

  • Explore the definition of mythology.
  • Gain an understanding of the nature and function of mythology.

As we begin our study of Greek and Roman Mythology, it is important first to lay some theoretical and historical groundwork which will serve as a foundation for our work during this semester. First, we will look at several theoretical approaches that help us to define and interpret Classical mythology. Second, we will proceed to an overview of Greek and Roman history and ancient approaches to religion, before undertaking a survey of the ancient sources we will be reading in the course.
