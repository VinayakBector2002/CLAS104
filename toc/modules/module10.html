Module 10

Athena, Ares, Hephaestus and Poseidon

Objectives:

  • Explore the nature and function of two gods closely connected to war, Athena/Minerva and Ares/Mars.
  • Examine the myths concerning Hephaestus/Vulcan, god of technology and craftsmanship.
 

We are going to look at Athena (in Latin Minerva), the goddess whose name is linked to the capital city of modern Greece. We will examine more briefly Ares (Mars), Hephaestus (Vulcan), and Poseidon (Neptune).
