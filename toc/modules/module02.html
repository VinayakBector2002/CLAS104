Module 02

The History Behind Classical Mythology

Objectives:

  • Consider in overview the major historical periods of the Greek and Roman worlds.
  • Examine various critical methods of interpreting mythology.

Before we start to think more about the gods of the Greeks and Romans, we will consider an overview of the history of these civilizations, in order to contextualize to some degree our understanding of the myths we are going to be examining. In particular, we will look at the following:

  • an overview of the major periods in Greek and Roman history
  • an introduction to the major Greek/Roman gods and polytheism

Essential Element: Full

Readings
