Module 06

Artemis and Virginity

Objectives:

  • Explore the nature and function of the goddess Artemis/Diana and central myths involving her.

We are going to look at Artemis, whose counterpart in Roman literature is Diana. Artemis is the daughter of Zeus and Leto, and famously the twin sister of the archer god Apollo.
