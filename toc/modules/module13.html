Module 13

Visions of Death: Hades and Hell

Objectives:

  • Examine the Greek and Roman concepts of death and the underworld through mythology.

In the second half of the course we are switching gears from the examination of individual gods to a broader consideration of Classical mythology. With our study of the gods providing a foundation, the second half of the course will take us through the story of the great battle of Troy, Odysseus’ long and fantastic journey home to his wife Penelope, and the great feats of Heracles (among other myths).
