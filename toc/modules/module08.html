Module 08

Demeter—Agrarian Mother and her Festivals

Objectives:

  • Explore the nature and function of the goddess Demeter/Ceres and the myth of the abduction of her daughter Persephone/Proserpina.

We are going to look in detail at Demeter, a goddess who is connected in literature and religious worship with the fertility of crops and the underworld. In this way Demeter is connected strongly to the earth, even as she also has a place in the pantheon of Olympian gods.
