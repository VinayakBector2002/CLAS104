Module 04

Zeus and the Pantheon

Objectives:

  • Explore the nature and function of the god Zeus/Jupiter and central myths involving him.

We are going to look in more detail at Zeus, who is, as we have already seen, the father of the gods in the Greek pantheon. We are first going to consider Zeus’ general characteristics and his role within mythology. But Zeus was also a god who was widely worshipped around the Greek world, holding a particularly prominent religious sanctuary in Olympia, where the Olympic games were held every four years. We will also consider in some detail the worship of Zeus at Olympia.
