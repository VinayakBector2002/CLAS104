Module 19

Theseus and the Minotaur

Objectives:

  • Explore the folktale narratives of Theseus' rise to power in Athens and his slaying of the Minotaur.

We will examine several of the myths surrounding the hero Theseus, most well known perhaps as the slayer of the Minotaur. Whereas Perseus, at whom we looked in the last Essential Element, was particularly associated with the Argolid, Theseus is linked prominently with Athens and the area surrounding it Attica. Theseus is in many ways a counterpart to Heracles, but is specifically the local hero of Attica and Athens.
