Textbook / Materials

Required

or

  • Classical Mythology (10th Ed.), M. Morford & R. Lenardon and M. Sham, Oxford, 2014.

Both editions can be used for this course, but you only need to purchase one of them.

  • Orpheus. Jean Cocteau, DVD, (1950, 2011)
