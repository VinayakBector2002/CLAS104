Essential Element: Part I

Heracles

Heracles is in myth strongly connected to Argos and Thebes, but his exploits stretch around the Greek world. Heracles is a remarkable hero for many reasons, but perhaps no more so than for the fact that he is one of the few mortals in Classical Mythology to be divinized (made fully divine and one of the immortals). In both literature and art Heracles, well known for his trademark lion-skin and club (see image 1), was a popular subject in antiquity.
